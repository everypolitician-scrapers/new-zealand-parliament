There are seven parliamentary parties presently represented in the 51st Parliament by 121 members of Parliament (currently there are only 120 members of Parliament as the Mt Roskill electorate seat is vacant pending a by-election on 3 December 2016).   These include 64 general electorate members, 7 members representing Māori electorates, and 50 members selected from party lists.
